[TITLE]
Tourvisto: An Insight-Driven SaaS Travel Management Platform

[DESCRIPTION]
Tourvisto is an enterprise-grade SaaS platform engineered to solve inefficiency in the travel industry by unifying client and admin operations into one system.

As the sole architect and developer, I delivered:
*   A secure, dual-role architecture via NextAuth.js middleware.
*   An intuitive booking portal for travelers.
*   A powerful admin dashboard with real-time data analytics.

This project showcases my expertise in the end-to-end development of complex, data-driven, and scalable applications.

Tech: Next.js 15, TypeScript, TailwindCSS, Recharts.
